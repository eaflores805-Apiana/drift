## What the numbers point at (observation, not prescription)

The packet asks: is the fix likely one global threshold adjustment, a highlight-route adjustment, a doorway route, a utility route, or some combination? The numbers above are consistent with the following:

### A. Global threshold drop — *insufficient on its own*

Reaches p008 if dropped to ~0.40. Does not reach p004 (would need ~0.20), p018 (~0.10), or p020/p025 (~0.07). A drop to 0.07 voices everything including p016 and p030 — i.e., voices the political rant and the generic promo. Cannot solve the labeled cluster as one knob.

### B. Highlight route — *helps p008 cleanly, partly helps p018*

p008: close (0.9) × life-event-magnitude (0.80) = the best raw inputs in the cluster. A small lift via relevance compute (Dana is in the listener's `closeness_map` and family; relevance should run high) or a multiplicative bump for `closeness=close AND magnitude≥0.7` lands p008 voiced without disturbing junk.

p018: high magnitude (0.65) but closeness is the *binding* constraint (followed = 0.3). Highlight route helps if it explicitly bumps closeness for `followed_entities` the listener actively follows (Buena High Athletics is in `listener.followed_entities`).

### C. Doorway route — *the only shape that addresses p004*

p004 has magnitude 0.45 with `sensitivity=high`. The current formula treats sensitivity as neutral in the score; in practice sensitive items "lose" because the model also rates their magnitude modestly. A doorway route that voices high-closeness × high-sensitivity items *with tight tone/specificity constraints* is the only mechanism in view that voices p004 without inviting close-friend false-voicing on lower-confidence sensitive content. The TL's "doorway" gold tag already encodes this; the formula does not yet.

### D. Utility route — *required for the followed-tier cluster*

The followed-tier closeness cap is mathematically dominant:

```
max possible raw score at closeness=0.3:
  mag(1.0) × closeness(0.3) × rel-boost(1.0) × time-boost(1.0)  =  0.300
max effective with focus=2.0:                                       0.600
```

So even a perfect followed-tier item — max magnitude, max relevance, max timeliness, max focus — can only just clear the voice threshold (0.6 > 0.45). **No realistic combination reaches voiced for followed-tier items with average meaning scores.** p018/p020/p025 all sit at modest mag, baseline relevance, varying timeliness, and they cluster at 0.07–0.11.

Plausible utility-route mechanics, in order of disruption to the existing formula:
1. **Per-entity relevance bump** — if `item.account_id` is in `listener.followed_entities` AND the entity matches a listener `interest`, override `relevance` from the 0.5 baseline to something like 0.9. (Driftwood follows + "specialty coffee" interest; Ventura News follows + local; Buena follows + "basketball"-adjacent.)
2. **Followed-tier closeness floor** — bump `followed = 0.5` instead of 0.3 when the entity is also in `listener.followed_entities` (vs. just appearing in `closeness_map`).
3. **Separate utility threshold** — lower threshold (e.g., 0.20) gated by `source_type ∈ {brand, local_org, news}` AND `expires_at != null` AND `entity in followed_entities`. Bypasses the multiplicative closeness cap entirely.

### E. Combination — *the data is most consistent with this*

- p008 → highlight route (or modest threshold drop)
- p004 → doorway route (the unique sensitive-close case)
- p018, p020, p025 → utility route (or any of the three followed-tier fixes above)

A single global tweak cannot reach all five without pulling junk in. The three clusters are distinct enough in shape that the team's earlier hypothesis of separate routes is now visible in the numbers.

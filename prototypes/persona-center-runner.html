Judge each take on four things

    
No score is computed here on purpose — the whole point is a human ear that isn't the author's. The bet only holds if a take is both of the last two at once.

    

      
Grounded
Zero claims the facts don't support. Watch for smuggled backstory — "landed," "finally," "dream job."

      
Safe
No invented interior. It never says how Dana feels or why it matters to her.

      
Recognizably the host
Warm, brief, music-bookended, points back to the listener — without being told to.

      
Alive
Would you want another break? Or is it safe-but-dead — correct and lifeless?
